Project Extension

In the instance of the project being funded for extended development, a dedicated team of four professionals will be employed to deliver the final project outcomes. The following jobs will be advertised to find the right team to continue development of this project.

Lead Developer

This is a technical role leading a small, focused group of professionals in the development of a community-based trading platform for a mobile application. You will be responsible for the output of your team as well as all aspects of the project, including development, integration and testing. You will work closely with your team of developers, and liase with business analysts and clients to ensure the project is delivered on time and within budget.


Key Responsibilities

  • Mentoring and monitoring development team
  • Leading creation of a growing product
  • Developing and implementing code standards and practices
  • Communication with client and management
  • Designing solutions and defining timeframes
  • Coordinating technical activities within the team
  • Leading product designing, coding, testing and deployment

Key Requirements

  • Ability to translate client vision into functional and feasible outcomes
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Excellent leadership skills
  • Strong problem-solving skills
  • Strong time-management and scheduling skills

Technical Requirements

  • Excellent understanding of MongoDB and Database management
  • Excellent understanding of Atlas App Services
  • Proficiency with GitHub
  • Understanding of mobile development
  • Extensive experience in JavaScript best practices
  • Understanding of UX Design
  • Experience in using open source and cloud based technologies



Back End Developer

This is a highly technical role in a small, focused group of professionals in the development of a community-based trading platform for a mobile application. You will be responsible for configuring and developing the back end of the application, using MongoDB databases and a variety of other drivers and services. Integration of several services is a vital part of this project, and proficiency across a range of fields is highly desired.


Key Responsibilities

  • Consistent reporting to lead developer on deadlines and work output
  • Configuration of database and continued management
  • Coding functions and triggers in accordance with standards and best practices
  • External service integration
  • Testing and debugging

Key Requirements

  • Ability to work efficiently and within timeframes
  • Ability to clearly communicate outputs
  • Solution based thinking
  • Focus and dedication to maintaining code standards

Technical Requirements

  • Excellent understanding of MongoDB and Database management
  • Excellent understanding of Atlas App Services
  • Proficiency with GitHub
  • Understanding of AWS, trimble maps, Socket.io
  • Understanding of mobile development
  • Experience in JavaScript best practices
  • Expert skills in building products using open source and cloud technologies



Front End Developer

This is a technical role in a small, focused group of professionals in the development of a community-based trading platform for a mobile application. You will be responsible for configuring and linking the front-end of the application, using JavaScript and MongoDB Atlas App Services. You will ideally have a strong understanding of mobile application interface design, and the ability to work independently within a small team to deliver focused outcomes.


Key Responsibilities

  • Consistent reporting to lead developer on deadlines and work output
  • Designing and iterating user-focused interface
  • Linking interface to back-end with JavaScript functionality
  • Coding functions in accordance with standards and best practices
  • Planning and performing UX evaluation to confirm accessibility requirements

Key Requirements

  • Ability to work efficiently and within timeframes
  • Ability to clearly communicate outputs
  • Solution based thinking
  • Focus and dedication to maintaining code standards
  • High adaptability and efficiency in work methods

Technical Requirements

  • Proficiency in JavaScript and mobile app interface development
  • Experience with prototyping in Figma
  • Strong design vision and UX expertise



Business Analyst

This is a technical role in a small, focused group of professionals in the development of a community-based trading platform for a mobile application. You will be responsible for communication with clients and lead developer regarding project feasibility and accessibility.


Key Responsibilities

  • Communicating with client and lead developer to ensure clarity on product outputs and project timelines
  • Working with development team to define required structures
  • Assessing market and product feasibility
  • Coordination marketing and product rollout
  • Ensuring cohesion across the team regarding expected deliverables to prevent scope creep
  • Maintaining risk assessments and mitigation strategies

Key Requirements

  • Excellent communication skills
  • Excellent leadership and oversight
  • Ability to adhere strictly to deadlines
  • Critical and analytical thinking

Technical Requirements

  • Able to develop deep understanding of the product and relevant tech
  • Proficiency in Excel in a business management setting
  • Experience in business management and data analysis
